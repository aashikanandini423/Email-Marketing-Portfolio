
# A/B Testing + Optimization Thinking

## Objective
Optimize email performance by testing subject lines and improving open rates.

## Business Problem
Low email open rates impacting campaign performance.

## Experiment Design

Variable Tested:
- Subject Line

Control Group (A):
"Get 20% Off Today"

Variant Group (B):
"Your Exclusive 20% Offer Awaits"

Audience Split:
- 50% Group A
- 50% Group B

## Results (Simulated)

| Metric | Version A | Version B |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Open Rate | 18% | 25% |
| CTR | 6% | 9% |

## Analysis
- Personalized tone increased engagement
- Urgency + exclusivity improved performance

## Key Learnings
- Subject lines significantly impact open rate
- A/B testing should be continuous
- Small changes lead to measurable improvements

## Optimization Recommendations
- Test send time
- Use personalization tokens
- Test CTA variations


## Concepts Used
- A/B Testing
- Campaign Analytics
- Performance Optimization
